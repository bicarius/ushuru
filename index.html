About Our App

Jembe is an app that offers Kenyans a comprehensive understanding of the various tax laws in the country. It is designed to educate users on the various tax regulations and help them stay compliant with the laws. The app features a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for users to navigate through the various tax laws and regulations.
One of the unique features of Jembe is its ability to allow users to comment and seek advice from verified tax experts. This feature is designed to help users better understand the tax laws and regulations, and to provide them with the necessary guidance to help them stay compliant.

Regular Updates

The app is regularly updated with the latest tax laws and regulations, ensuring that users have access to the most accurate and up-to-date information.

Educational Resources

Additionally, Jembe offers a variety of educational resources, including articles, videos, and interactive quizzes, that users can use to improve their knowledge of tax laws.

Tax Consulting

Jembe is an app that permits tax consulting from experts, allowing users to easily seek advice and guidance on tax-related matters. These tax experts are verified and certified.

Interactive

The app also allows for interactivity between users through commenting on various tax clauses, making it a valuable tool for building a community of informed tax payers.

Freemium Model

Jembe operates on a freemium model, offering users a free version of the app with basic features, and a paid version with additional features and allowing users to try out the app before committing to a paid subscription.

Offline Support

Additionally, Jembe supports essential features such as allowing users to view tax clauses in offline mode, making it accessible even in areas with limited or no internet connectivity.
